Standard Goods Re-design

Client: TheStandardGoods.com

Standard Goods is a boutique in Capitol Hill, selling unique designer collections. They specialize on products crafted in North America.

Duration : 2 weeks sprint

Process : Research, Competitive analysis, Task Analysis, Affinity diagramming , Customer Journey, Sketching, Storyboarding, Card sort, Site map, Wireframes, User Interface Design, Rapid Prototyping, User Testing.

Tools : Photoshop, Omnigraffle and InVision.

View Prototype





Challenge:

They lack web presence in terms of online shopping. There is no easy way to browse the products as they are not grouped properly. By improving the Information Architecture, StandardGoods is hoping to improve the product search, promote and increase online sales. For this individual project , I had to identify the following: Better and meaningful ways to organize and group products (Information Architecture). Where in the process of purchasing a product, an user gets stuck. How to engage and guide the users in completing the purchase.
